National Science and Technology Fellowship 2022–2023 2022–2023

Ministry of Science and Technology, Government of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh.

My master's thesis, titled "Design of Sylhet City as a Sponge City to Control Flood Water," was recognized by the Ministry of Science and Technology, Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh. This research was awarded the prestigious National Science and Technology Fellowship. The study focused on innovative solutions to urban flooding through the integration of sponge city principles, utilizing advanced methodologies such as remote sensing and machine learning. It aimed to develop sustainable urban infrastructures that enhance resilience against flood risks while promoting ecological balance in urban environments.
